OUR LATEST KITCHEN REMODEL

Transforming a Beautiful Kitchen
Full Appliance Package and Custom Cabinetry

We began this project by modifying the cabinets to install both a Pro Range and a Pro Vent Hood in the center of this wall of cabinets. Then, the wall ovens to the left were removed, and we transformed the space into a custom appliance garage, complete with a rollout drawer and an outlet as well as retractable doors for easy access. After that, we heavily modified the cabinets on the right by lifting the top and replacing the doors to fit the built-in fridge before we attached the beautiful custom-built and color-matched fridge panel.

Custom Appliance Garage
A Functional Transformation of Space

Here you can see where the old wall ovens were previously installed. We finished the inside of the cabinet, added rollout drawer boxes complete with custom fronts, installed the special hinges for the new custom-built doors, and had the outlet moved for easy accessbility.

Installing the Built-In Fridge
Major Cabinet Modification

The previous fridge was much shorter than the new built-in fridge. Because of this height difference, the upper cabinets needed to be modified so that the new fridge could be installed. So we lifted the cabinet deck up and fabricated new doors to match the front. We also created the custom fridge panel that we then attached to the front of the fridge to complete the look.

The Final Result
One Awesome Kitchen Remodel

This beautiful kitchen has received quite the upgrade! Professional appliances were added, old appliances were removed, and custom cabinets were designed, built and installed. Our team is thrilled with the outcome in this kitchen and is looking forward to the next project!
